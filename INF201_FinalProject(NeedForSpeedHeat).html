Need For Speed Heat

Need for Speed Heat (stylized as NFS Heat) is a racing video game. The Need For Speed franchise has been around for a long time. It is the twenty-fourth installment in the Need for Speed series and commemorates the series' 25th anniversary. It was released on November 8, 2019. The game offers a singleplayer mode and an online mode where you can race online or with friends. This is the game for you if you are into racing.

Developers: EA Gothenburg

Platforms: Playstation 4, Xbox One, Microsoft Windows
