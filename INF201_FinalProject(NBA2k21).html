NBA 2k21

NBA 2K21 is a basketball simulation video game. This game is based on the National Basketball Association (NBA). It is the 22nd installment in the NBA 2K franchise and the successor to NBA 2K20. The game was released on September 4, 2020. It will later be released for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X and Series S. It offers a single player mode where you can create a basketball player. There is also an online mode where you can play online or with friends. This is the game for you if you are into sports
