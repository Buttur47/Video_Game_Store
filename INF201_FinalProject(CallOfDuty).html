Call Of Duty Modern Warfare(2019)

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is a 2019 first-person shooter video game. Serving as the sixteenth overall installment in the Call of Duty series, as well as a reboot of the Modern Warfare sub-series. It was released on October 25, 2019. It. offers a campaign mode with an interesting story. There is also Multiplayer, Co-op, and Warzone. This is the game for you if you are into shooters.

Developers: Infinity Ward, Sledgehammer Games

Awards: The Game Award for Best Audio Design

Platforms: PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Microsoft Windows

Price: $60.00
